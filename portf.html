Hungarian Innovation Ecosystem Map
About the project

The Hungarian National Innovation Agency previously had a basic, early-stage user interface for their Hungarian Innovation Ecosystem Map. They launched a call to redesign the site, aiming to make it more intuitive, interactive, and enjoyable to use. Our team, consisting of Anna Nina Tötő, Luca Sára Kiss, and myself, took on the challenge and collaborated to create a more engaging and user-friendly design.

 

UX design
UI design
During the redesign process, we aimed to create a transparent, aesthetically modern, and intuitive user experience, where visual appeal and content emphasis seamlessly intertwine.
Spacious layout: Improved readability with less information overload and a clean, uncluttered feel.
Icons: Help users understand content more quickly and intuitively through visual cues.
Hierarchy and consistency: Visually distinct, well-structured sections make indicators and focus areas easier to grasp at a glance.
